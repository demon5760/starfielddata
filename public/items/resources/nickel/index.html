Nickel

Nickel is a Common Inorganic resource. One Nickel has a mass of 0.6 and is worth 10 credits. It has a symbol of Ni and can be stored in Solid containers.

  • Purchase
  • Production
    • Extractor - Nickel
    • Extractor - Nickel - Commercial
    • Extractor - Nickel - Industrial

Purchase

Nickel can be purchased at the following locations.

  • Jae Montreal at New Homestead on Titan in Sol (Store)
