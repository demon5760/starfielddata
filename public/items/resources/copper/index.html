Copper

Copper is a Common Inorganic resource. One Copper has a mass of 0.6 and is worth 10 credits. It has a symbol of Cu and can be stored in Solid containers.

  • Purchase
  • Production

    • Extractor - Copper - Commercial
    • Extractor - Copper - Industrial

Purchase

Copper can be purchased at the following locations.

  • Jae Montreal at New Homestead on Titan in Sol (Store)
