Platinum

Platinum is a Rare Inorganic resource. One Platinum has a mass of 0.8 and is worth 24 credits. It has a symbol of Pt and can be stored in Solid containers.

Platinum can be purchased at the following locations.

  • Jae Montreal at New Homestead on Titan in Sol (Store)
