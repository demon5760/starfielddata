Lead

Lead is a Common Inorganic resource. One Lead has a mass of 0.7 and is worth 10 credits. It has a symbol of Pb and can be stored in Solid containers.

  • Production

Purchase

Lead can be purchased at the following locations.

  • Jae Montreal at New Homestead on Titan in Sol (Store)
