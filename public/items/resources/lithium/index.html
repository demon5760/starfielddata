Lithium

Lithium is a Rare Inorganic resource. One Lithium has a mass of 0.5 and is worth 14 credits. It has a symbol of Li and can be stored in Solid containers.

Purchase

Lithium can be purchased at the following locations.

  • Jae Montreal at New Homestead on Titan in Sol (Store)
